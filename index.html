Hello, I'm Lydia. I develop sites.

lydia hendriks

I'm about creating stunning + efficient sites that are natural + fun to interact with. Using modern web technologies to keep sites clean + maintainable.

I'm a full stack developer based out of Tampa, FL. I love bringing life to stunning designs that promote a fluid user experience.

My background is in management consulting, which gave me ample experience working with stakeholders to understand business needs and design solutions. I repeatedly saw that 1.) web applications were the answers to many of my clients' problems and 2.) many organizations are in desperate need of more web capabilities.

As a craftsman at heart, I love building projects from scratch and want to understand exactly how things work 'under the hood', so to speak. So naturally, I found working with CMS too limiting and inefficient. I started to notice bloat, inefficiency, and poor design (triple cringe!). At that point, I started learning front-end fundamentals and immediately fell in love with building web applications.
